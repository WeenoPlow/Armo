Our Games


Armogaste SARL is a startup dedicated to the creation of mobile games and social apps. Since October 2014, we've delivered 8 apps on the three major stores (Appstore, Google Play, Windows Phone). Our portfolio includes different types of games : Arcade, Board games, Strategy, Educational, Musical and Social Apps. We offer competitive rates for the creation of such apps. We work quickly, and efficiently in a creative atmosphere with partners such as WWSight and IngeSup. Thanks to a close proximity and relation to the School of Ingesup we have access to dedicated developers and motivated students. Armogaste is growing and is extending its professional expertise. We are looking for more games to develop, but also would enjoy creating utility apps. In a growing market of increasing expenses, we deliver a transparent budget, a clear outline of development and excellent quality for your product.
